A Simple Hashi Puzzle

APRIL 13, 2024 | TYPESCRIPT, SOLIDJS, PUZZLES

About

Hashiwokakero - known as "building bridges" in English - are deductive logic puzzles consisting of "islands" connected together with "bridges". The rules are simple:

  • islands have a number of bridges that should connect to it
  • bridges can only be built horizontally or vertically
  • bridges cannot cross eachother
  • up to two bridges can be built in the same connection
  • all islands must be interconnected with eachother
